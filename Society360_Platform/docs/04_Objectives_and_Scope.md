# Objectives and Scope

## Project Objectives

The primary objective of the Society360 â€“ Smart Residential Management System is to design and develop a secure, scalable, and user-friendly digital platform that efficiently manages residential society operations.

The specific objectives of the project are:

- To provide secure, role-based access for residents, management, and facility staff
- To digitize visitor management and gate entry processes
- To streamline maintenance request submission, assignment, and tracking
- To implement a transparent maintenance billing and payment system

## Project Scope

The scope of Society360 includes the design and implementation of the following functional areas:

### In-Scope Features

#### User Management
- User registration and authentication
- Role-based access control (Resident, Admin, Staff)
- Unit and resident profile management

#### Visitor & Gate Management
- Visitor pre-approval by residents
- Real-time entry and exit logging by security staff
- Visitor history and audit logs

#### Maintenance & Complaint Management
- Maintenance and grievance submission by residents
- Task assignment and status updates by management
- Real-time status tracking and resolution logging

#### Finance & Billing
- Monthly maintenance bill generation
- Simulated digital payment processing
- Digital receipt generation
- Basic financial reports for administrators

#### Communication & Announcements
- Official announcements by management
- Resident notifications for important events
- Controlled discussion or communication channels

#### Administration & Reporting
- User, unit, and system configuration management
- Reports on payments, maintenance performance, and visitor activity

---

## Out-of-Scope Features

The following features are explicitly excluded from the current project scope:

- Real-time payment gateway integration with banks or UPI systems
- Advanced IoT-based access control or surveillance systems
- Legal document management or compliance automation
- AI-based prediction or analytics modules
- Native mobile application development (web-responsive design only)

---

## Project Constraints

- Strict adherence to Civora Nexus Pvt. Ltd. branding and design guidelines
- Use of only approved technologies and official assets
- Simulated payment processing for financial transactions
- Development limited to the internship duration and academic timeline
